We are the Willow Tree, an online store based out of Ottawa Ontario Canada. As a company, we specialize in providing a variety of quality clothing accessories, including jewelry, eyewear, and footwear to individuals all over the world.

We opened our doors in 2006 and have been overflowing with business ever since. Each of our products is drawn-up and created with care in Canada by hard-working and passionate designers.
